<p><p>Ostomy care and accessories encompass products designed to support individuals with stomas. One-piece ostomy products integrate the pouch and wafer into a single unit, offering ease of application and a discreet profile. They are ideal for active users seeking convenience. Conversely, traditional two-piece ostomy products feature a separate pouch and skin barrier, allowing for flexibility in changing pouches while keeping the barrier in place. This option is favored for its versatility and adaptability to varied user needs and preferences.</p></p>
