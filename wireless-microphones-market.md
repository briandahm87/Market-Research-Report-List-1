<p><p>The wireless microphones market is segmented into three main types: handheld, clip-on, and other wireless microphones. Handheld wireless microphones are portable and commonly used in live performances and public speaking, allowing for mobility and ease of use. Clip-on wireless microphones, also known as lavalier microphones, are small and attach directly to clothing, providing discreet sound capture for interviews, presentations, or video production. The "other" category encompasses various specialized wireless microphones, including headsets and boundary microphones, catering to diverse applications in entertainment and professional fields.</p></p>
