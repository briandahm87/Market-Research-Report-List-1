<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Collimators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multi-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic collimators market is categorized into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode collimators. Single-mode collimators utilize a narrow core diameter, allowing only one light mode to propagate, resulting in higher bandwidth and longer transmission distances, making them ideal for telecommunications and long-range applications. Conversely, multi-mode collimators feature a larger core diameter, enabling multiple light modes to transmit simultaneously. They are typically used for short-distance applications like data centers and local area networks, offering greater capacity over shorter distances.</p></p>
