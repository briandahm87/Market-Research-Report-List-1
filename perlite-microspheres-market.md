<p><p>The Perlite Microspheres market is primarily divided into two main types: water-based and resin-based microspheres. Water-based microspheres utilize a water-soluble medium, making them eco-friendly and suitable for applications in paints, coatings, and construction products. They offer good insulation and lightweight properties. Resin-based microspheres, on the other hand, are designed for durability and strength, commonly used in industrial applications such as automotive and aerospace components, providing enhanced mechanical properties and thermal resistance. Each type caters to distinct needs in various industries.</p></p>
