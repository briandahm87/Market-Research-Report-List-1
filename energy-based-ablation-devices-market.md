<p><p>The Energy-Based Ablation Devices market encompasses various technologies used for tissue destruction in medical procedures. Electrical ablation uses currents to remove or destroy tissue. Light-based devices utilize lasers for precision targeting. Radiation ablation employs ionizing radiation to disrupt cellular structures. Radiofrequency ablation generates heat to achieve tissue necrosis, while ultrasound ablation uses high-frequency sound waves for tissue disruption. Other types may include cryo-ablation or microwave ablation, each offering distinct advantages for specific clinical applications and patient needs.</p></p>
