<p><strong>The Recycled CFRP Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Process</li><li>Chemical Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP) market can be categorized into two main processes: physical and chemical. The physical process involves mechanical methods, such as grinding and milling, to reclaim carbon fibers from composite materials while maintaining their structural properties. In contrast, the chemical process employs solvents or high-temperature treatments to break down the polymer matrix, allowing for the extraction of pure carbon fibers. Both methods aim to enhance sustainability by reducing waste and promoting resource recovery in various industries.</p></p>
