<p><p>The Heavy Industry Gas Detectors market comprises two main types: stationary and portable. Stationary gas detectors are fixed installations designed for continuous monitoring in industrial settings, providing real-time data and alarms for gas leaks. They are ideal for high-risk areas requiring constant surveillance. Portable gas detectors, on the other hand, are handheld devices used for on-the-go monitoring, allowing workers to assess air quality in various locations, particularly in confined spaces or during maintenance activities. Both types enhance safety and compliance in heavy industries.</p></p>
