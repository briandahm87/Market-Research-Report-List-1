<p><p>The aircraft market can be categorized into three main types: propeller aircraft, turbo-prop aircraft, and jet aircraft. Propeller aircraft, often smaller and used for short-haul flights, rely on piston engines to generate thrust. Turbo-prop aircraft utilize turbine engines to drive propellers, offering better speed and efficiency for regional travel. Jet aircraft, equipped with jet engines, provide high-speed and long-range capabilities, making them ideal for commercial airlines and cargo operations. Each type serves distinct operational needs in aviation.</p></p>
