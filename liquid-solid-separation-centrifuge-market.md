<p><ul><li>Two-phase Separator</li><li>Three-phase Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid-solid separation centrifuge market comprises two primary types: two-phase separators and three-phase separators. Two-phase separators are designed to separate liquid and solid phases, effectively removing solids from liquids in various applications, such as wastewater treatment and food processing. In contrast, three-phase separators are utilized to separate three distinct phases: typically liquid, solid, and a second liquid phase (such as oil and water). These are commonly used in oil and gas industries, as well as in chemical processing, to achieve efficient separation and recovery of resources.</p></p>
