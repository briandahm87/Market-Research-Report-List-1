<p><p>The electric and hybrid aircraft propulsion system market consists of two primary types: hybrid powertrains and all-electric powertrains. Hybrid powertrains combine conventional engines with electric motors, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions, making them suitable for longer ranges. In contrast, all-electric powertrains utilize only electric propulsion, offering zero emissions and lower operational costs but typically limited by battery capacity. Both systems aim to enhance sustainability and operational efficiency in aviation, responding to growing environmental concerns and technological advancements.</p></p>
