<p><strong>The Industrial Centrifuges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sedimentation Centrifuges</li><li>Filtering Centrifuges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial centrifuge market comprises primarily two types: sedimentation centrifuges and filtering centrifuges. Sedimentation centrifuges utilize centrifugal force to separate substances based on density differences, effectively separating solids from liquids in various industries. In contrast, filtering centrifuges employ a filter medium to separate solids from fluids, making them ideal for applications requiring clarified liquids. Both types are essential in sectors like pharmaceuticals, food processing, and wastewater treatment, enhancing efficiency and productivity in separation processes.</p></p>
