<p><p>The dispensing nozzles market consists of three primary types: ceramic, metallic, and plastic nozzles. Metallic nozzles, often made from stainless steel, offer strength and thermal conductivity, suitable for high-temperature operations. Plastic nozzles are lightweight and cost-effective, commonly used in low-pressure applications and for dispensing viscous materials. Each type caters to specific industry needs, driving demand across various sectors.</p></p>
