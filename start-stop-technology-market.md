<p><ul><li>Enhanced starter</li><li>Belt-driven alternator starter (BAS)</li><li>Direct starter</li><li>Integrated starter generator (ISG)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Start-stop technology enhances fuel efficiency by automatically shutting off the engine during idle and restarting it when needed. The market features several types: </p><p>1. **Enhanced Starter**: Upgraded conventional starters designed for frequent operation.</p><p>2. **Belt-driven Alternator Starter (BAS)**: Combines starter and alternator functions, improving energy recovery during braking.</p><p>3. **Direct Starter**: Directly engages the engine, offering quick restarts.</p><p>4. **Integrated Starter Generator (ISG)**: Combines starter and generator roles in a single unit, maximizing efficiency and performance in hybrid and electric vehicles.</p></p>
