<p><p>The roll-formed racking market primarily features two types of post designs: C-shape posts and closed-tube posts. C-shape posts are characterized by their open channel design, allowing for easy integration of shelving and accessories, while providing adequate strength and stability. Closed-tube posts, on the other hand, offer enhanced rigidity and structural integrity due to their enclosed design, making them suitable for heavier loads. Both types cater to various warehousing and storage needs, influencing efficiency and space utilization.</p></p>
