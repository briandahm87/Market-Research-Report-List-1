<p><p>The Underwater Drive System market is categorized into three main types: Outboard, Flange Motor, and Other. Outboard systems are external motors mounted on the transom of boats, providing flexibility and ease of maintenance. Flange Motors are integrated directly into the vessel’s structure for a more compact design, optimizing space and efficiency. The "Other" category encompasses various innovative systems, such as directly driven propulsors and turbine drives, catering to niche applications and specialized marine needs.</p></p>
