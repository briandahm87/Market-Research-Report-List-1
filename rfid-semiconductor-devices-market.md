<p><p>The RFID semiconductor devices market encompasses various components essential for radio frequency communication. RF filters eliminate unwanted signals, ensuring clear data transmission. RF switches control signal paths to enhance device versatility. RF power amplifiers boost signal strength for extended range, while RF duplexers enable simultaneous transmission and reception. RF modulators convert digital data into radio signals, whereas demodulators perform the inverse, extracting information from received signals. Together, these components play a critical role in the efficiency and performance of RFID systems.</p></p>
