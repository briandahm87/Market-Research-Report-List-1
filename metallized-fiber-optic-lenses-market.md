<p><p>The metallized fiber optic lenses market comprises two primary types: single-mode fiber and multimode fiber. Single-mode fiber is designed for long-distance transmission, utilizing a single light path for minimal signal loss and high bandwidth. In contrast, multimode fiber allows multiple light paths, making it suitable for shorter distances with higher light throughput. Each type has distinct applications, influencing the demand for metallized lenses tailored for specific functionalities, performance characteristics, and integration into fiber optic systems.</p></p>
