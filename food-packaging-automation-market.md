<p><p>The food packaging automation market is divided into two main types: primary packaging and secondary packaging. Primary packaging refers to the direct containment of the food product, typically involving materials like plastic, glass, or metal that protect the item and ensure freshness. Secondary packaging, on the other hand, involves additional layers that group primary packages for distribution, marketing, or convenience, often using boxes or shrink-wrap. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce waste, and improve product safety throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
