<p><p>The HV bushing market can be categorized into two main types: condenser and non-condenser types. </p><p>Condenser-type bushings utilize a capacitance mechanism to enhance electrical performance and efficiency, making them suitable for high-voltage applications, particularly in transformers and switching stations. </p><p>In contrast, non-condenser type bushings are simpler in design, relying on insulation materials to withstand high voltages without the added complexity of capacitance. Both types serve critical roles in ensuring safe and reliable electrical transmission in power systems.</p></p>
