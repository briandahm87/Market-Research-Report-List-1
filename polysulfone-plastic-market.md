<p><p>The polysulfone plastic market is categorized into three primary types: Polysulfone (PSU), Polyethersulfone (PES), and Polyphenylsulfone (PPSU). PSU is known for its thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it suitable for various applications in the automotive and aerospace sectors. PES offers enhanced chemical resistance and high-temperature performance, often used in medical devices and filtration systems. PPSU provides exceptional toughness and impact resistance, commonly utilized in healthcare and food processing industries, thus addressing diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
