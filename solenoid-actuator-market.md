<p><p>A solenoid actuator is an electromechanical device that converts electrical energy into linear motion. It consists of a coil of wire, a movable plunger or core, and a spring. When electric current passes through the coil, it generates a magnetic field that pulls the plunger into the coil, creating movement. Solenoid actuators are widely used in various applications, including automotive systems, industrial automation, locking mechanisms, and home appliances due to their reliability and rapid response times.</p><p>The solenoid actuator market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing automation across industries and the rising demand for efficient and compact mechanisms.
