<p><p>The modified calcium carbonate market is categorized into heavy and light calcium-base materials, based on their production processes and applications. Heavy calcium carbonate, derived from natural calcite or limestone, offers greater particle size and is widely used in industries like construction, plastics, and paints due to its high purity and density. In contrast, light calcium carbonate is produced through a chemical process, resulting in smaller, more uniform particles. It finds applications in the food, pharmaceutical, and cosmetic industries for its brightness and opacity.</p></p>
