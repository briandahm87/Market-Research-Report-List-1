<p><p>The carbon fiber woven cloth market is characterized by three primary weaving types. Unidirectional weaving aligns fibers in a single direction, enhancing strength and stiffness for specific applications. Two-way weaving interlaces fibers at right angles, providing balanced mechanical properties and versatility for various products. Multiaxial weaving incorporates fibers in multiple directions, delivering superior structural integrity and resistance to forces, making it ideal for complex and high-performance applications. Each type caters to distinct requirements in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
