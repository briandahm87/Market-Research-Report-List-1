<p><strong>The Liquid Level Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Level Instrumentation</li><li>Point Level Instrumentation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid level measurement market comprises two primary types: continuous level instrumentation and point level instrumentation. Continuous level instrumentation provides real-time measurements of liquid levels within a tank or vessel, utilizing technologies such as radar, ultrasonic, or capacitance methods. In contrast, point level instrumentation is designed to detect specific points within a container, triggering alerts or actions when the level reaches a predetermined threshold. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing processes and ensuring safety in various industrial applications.</p></p>
