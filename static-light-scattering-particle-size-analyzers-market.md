<p><p>The Static Light Scattering Particle Size Analyzer market is divided into two primary types: Wet Method and Dry Method. The Wet Method involves dispersing particles in a liquid medium, allowing for precise size measurement through light scattering in a controlled environment. Conversely, the Dry Method analyzes particles in a gas phase, offering advantages for powder materials, providing rapid measurements without the need for solvents. Both methods cater to diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, materials science, and food processing, facilitating accurate particle size distribution analysis.</p></p>
