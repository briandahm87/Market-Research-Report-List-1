<p><p>Pressure labels, also known as pressure-sensitive labels, are self-adhesive labels that stick to surfaces upon application of light pressure. These labels do not require heat, water, or solvents for activation, making them a convenient and cost-effective labeling solution.</p><p>The Pressure Labels Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for packaged goods, advancements in labeling technologies, and the rise of e-commerce. These trends are shaping the future of the pressure labels market, making it a dynamic and rapidly evolving industry.</p></p>

<p><p>The Pressure Labels Market includes two main types: Release Liner Labels and Linerless Labels. Release Liner Labels consist of a face stock, adhesive, and a silicone-coated liner that is removed during application. They are widely used in industries like food, beverage, and pharmaceuticals due to their durability and ease of use. Linerless Labels, on the other hand, eliminate the liner, reducing waste and costs. They are self-adhesive and often used in retail and logistics for their eco-friendly and efficient design. Both types cater to diverse labeling needs.</p></p>
