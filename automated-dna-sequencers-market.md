<p><strong>The Automated DNA Sequencers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing</li><li>Next Generation DNA Sequencing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automated DNA Sequencers market is primarily divided into two types: Chain Terminator DNA Sequencing and Next Generation DNA Sequencing (NGS). Chain Terminator Sequencing, often referred to as Sanger sequencing, uses dideoxynucleotides to terminate DNA strand elongation, enabling accurate sequencing of small fragments. In contrast, NGS utilizes parallel processing to rapidly sequence millions of fragments simultaneously, greatly increasing throughput and efficiency. Both methodologies cater to diverse applications in genomics, diagnostics, and research, influencing advancements in personalized medicine and genetic studies.</p></p>
