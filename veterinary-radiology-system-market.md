<p><p>The veterinary radiology system market comprises two main types: digital X-ray systems and analog X-ray systems. Digital X-ray systems utilize advanced imaging technology, providing immediate results, enhanced image quality, and easier storage and sharing. They improve diagnostic efficiency and reduce radiation exposure. On the other hand, analog X-ray systems rely on traditional film-based methods, offering a lower initial cost but requiring more time for image development and processing. The choice between these systems depends on factors such as budget, diagnostic needs, and workflow preferences in veterinary practices.</p></p>
