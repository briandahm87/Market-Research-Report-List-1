<p><strong>The Organ Transplant Diagnostics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molecular Assay Technologies</li><li>Non-molecular Assay Technologies</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organ transplant diagnostics market is categorized into two main types: molecular assay technologies and non-molecular assay technologies. Molecular assay technologies involve techniques like polymerase chain reaction (PCR) and next-generation sequencing, which analyze genetic material for donor-recipient compatibility and rejection risk. In contrast, non-molecular assay technologies include serological tests and enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISAs) that measure antibodies and cytokines to assess immune responses. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring successful transplant outcomes through accurate diagnostics.</p></p>
