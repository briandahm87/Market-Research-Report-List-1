<p><p>The dive undersuits market is primarily divided into two main types: split dive undersuits and one-piece dive undersuits. Split dive undersuits consist of separate top and bottom pieces, offering flexibility and ease of movement, making them suitable for layered thermal protection. On the other hand, one-piece dive undersuits provide a seamless design, ensuring optimal insulation and reducing water entry, ideal for colder conditions. Both types cater to divers' needs for comfort, warmth, and mobility in underwater environments.</p></p>
