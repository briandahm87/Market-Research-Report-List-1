<p><p>The Pneumatic Power Clamps market includes two main types: Temporary Type and Permanent Type. Temporary Type clamps are designed for applications requiring frequent adjustments or repositioning, offering flexibility and ease of use in dynamic environments. They are ideal for tasks like assembly or welding. Permanent Type clamps are used for fixed, long-term applications where stability and consistent clamping force are critical, such as in heavy-duty manufacturing.
