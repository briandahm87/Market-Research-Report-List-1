<p><ul><li>Monocrystalline Ingots</li><li>Polycrystalline Ingots</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar silicon ingots market primarily consists of two types: monocrystalline and polycrystalline ingots. Monocrystalline ingots, made from a single crystal structure, offer higher efficiency and better performance in low-light conditions, making them ideal for space-constrained applications. In contrast, polycrystalline ingots are composed of multiple crystal structures, typically resulting in lower production costs but decreased efficiency compared to their monocrystalline counterparts. Both types play crucial roles in solar cell manufacturing, catering to diverse consumer needs and market preferences.</p></p>
