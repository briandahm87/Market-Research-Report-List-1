<p><p>The AC clamp meters market can be categorized into two main types: AC Voltage and AC Current. AC Voltage clamp meters are designed to measure the voltage in alternating current systems, providing accurate readings without direct contact. These are essential for troubleshooting and maintaining electrical systems. On the other hand, AC Current clamp meters measure the flow of electric current through conductors using a non-invasive clamp mechanism. Both types are vital for electricians and technicians to ensure safety and reliability in electrical applications.</p></p>
