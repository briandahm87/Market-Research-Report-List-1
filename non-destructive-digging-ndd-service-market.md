<p><p>The Non-Destructive Digging (NDD) service market encompasses various excavation types, primarily including hydraulic excavation and hydraulic and dry suction excavation. Hydraulic excavation uses high-pressure water jets to displace soil and debris without damaging underground utilities. In contrast, hydraulic and dry suction excavation combines hydraulic forces with suction technology to safely remove excavated materials, minimizing environmental impact. Both methods enhance safety and efficiency in urban construction and utility maintenance, preserving existing infrastructure while allowing for precise digging.</p></p>
