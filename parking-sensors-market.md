<p><p>The parking sensors market comprises mainly two types: electromagnetic and ultrasonic reversing sensors. Electromagnetic parking sensors create an electromagnetic field around the vehicle, alerting drivers to nearby obstacles without physical contact. In contrast, ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves that bounce off objects, detecting their distance and position. Both systems enhance parking safety and convenience, catering to the growing demand for advanced driver assistance technologies. Their integration in vehicles aids in minimizing accidents and improving maneuverability in tight spaces.</p></p>
