<p><p>Roll-to-Roll (R2R) technology encompasses various printing techniques used for producing printed materials on flexible substrates. Flexographic printing employs fast-drying inks, making it suitable for high-volume production of packaging materials. Gravure printing, with its high-quality output, is commonly used for long runs of detailed images. Other methods may include specialty or digital techniques.</p></p>
