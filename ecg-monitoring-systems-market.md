<p><strong>The ECG Monitoring Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Holter Monitors</li><li>Resting ECG System</li><li>Stress ECG Monitors</li><li>Event Monitoring Systems</li><li>ECG Management System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG monitoring systems market encompasses various types designed for different monitoring needs. Holter monitors provide continuous recording over 24-48 hours, useful for detecting intermittent cardiac issues. Resting ECG systems capture heart activity during a stationary state, often in clinical settings. Stress ECG monitors assess heart function during physical exertion, aiding in evaluating heart diseases. Event monitoring systems record ECG data triggered by patient symptoms, while ECG management systems streamline data storage, analysis, and reporting for improved clinical workflows.</p></p>
