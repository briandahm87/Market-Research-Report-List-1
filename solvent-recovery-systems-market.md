<p><p>Solvent recovery systems are categorized based on the type of pump used in the process. Vacuum pump solvent recovery systems utilize low-pressure environments to condense and recover solvents, enhancing efficiency. Centrifugal pump solvent recovery systems employ high-speed rotation to separate solvents, making them suitable for larger-scale operations. Vortex pump solvent recovery systems create a swirling flow to effectively separate solvents from contaminants. Other systems may include diaphragm or peristaltic pumps, catering to specific application needs across various industries.</p></p>
