<p><p>The HVAC temperature sensor market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. NTC sensors offer high sensitivity for low temperatures, while PTC sensors provide overcurrent protection and improve safety. RTD sensors deliver accurate readings and stability, making them ideal for critical environments. Thermocouples (TCs) are robust and versatile, suitable for extreme temperatures. Additionally, other sensors, like infrared and bimetallic sensors, cater to specialized needs. Together, these technologies enhance HVAC efficiency, control, and reliability in various settings.</p></p>
