<p><p>The silicone rubber additives market encompasses various types, each serving specific functions in rubber manufacturing. Dispersants improve the uniform distribution of fillers, enhancing material properties. Thickening agents increase viscosity, aiding in processing and application. Defoamers reduce air bubbles during mixing, promoting smoother surfaces. Adhesives strengthen bonding between different materials, ensuring durability and performance. Other additives may include curing agents, catalysts, and anti-aging substances, collectively contributing to the versatility and performance of silicone rubber in diverse applications.</p></p>
