<p><p>The automatic shut-off valves market comprises two primary types: hydraulic and electric. Hydraulic shut-off valves operate using fluid pressure to control flow, making them suitable for high-pressure applications and providing reliable performance in industrial settings. Electric shut-off valves rely on electrical signals to operate, allowing for precise control and integration with automation systems. Both types serve critical roles in preventing leaks, ensuring safety, and maintaining operational efficiency across various industries, including oil and gas, water management, and manufacturing.</p></p>
