<p><p>The automated immunoassay analyzers market comprises various types, including enhanced chemiluminescence and chemiluminescence assays. Enhanced chemiluminescence (ECL) employs light-emitting reactions to provide higher sensitivity and rapid results, making it suitable for detecting low-abundance targets. Traditional chemiluminescence assays also utilize light emission but may offer lower sensitivity. Both methods are integral in clinical diagnostics, enabling precise detection of biomarkers in areas like infectious disease testing, hormone levels, and cancer markers, thereby improving patient care and accelerating diagnostic processes.</p></p>
