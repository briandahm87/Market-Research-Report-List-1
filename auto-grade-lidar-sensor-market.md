<p><p>The Auto Grade LiDAR sensor market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical LiDAR and solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR utilizes rotating components to scan the environment, offering high-resolution 3D mapping but may be more susceptible to wear and environmental conditions. In contrast, solid-state LiDAR employs fixed components with no moving parts, resulting in increased durability and reduced costs while maintaining decent performance. Each type serves critical roles in autonomous vehicles, enhancing navigation, perception, and safety capabilities.</p></p>
