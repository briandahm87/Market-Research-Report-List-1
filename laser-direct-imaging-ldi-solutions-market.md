<p><strong>The Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygon Mirror 365nm</li><li>DMD 405nm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) Solutions market is characterized by different technologies, notably the Polygon Mirror 365nm and DMD 405nm. The Polygon Mirror 365nm technology utilizes a rotating mirror to reflect laser light for high-resolution imaging of circuit patterns, enhancing precision in PCB production. On the other hand, the DMD 405nm (Digital Micromirror Device) employs a micro-mirror array to modulate laser light, offering flexibility and speed in high-quality imaging applications. Both types contribute significantly to advancements in photolithography techniques.</p></p>
