<p><p>The basement waterproofing membranes market includes various types, each offering unique properties. Bitumen membranes are durable and provide excellent waterproofing, often used in commercial applications. PVC membranes are flexible, lightweight, and resistant to chemicals. TPO membranes are known for their energy efficiency and UV resistance. EPDM is a synthetic rubber membrane that excels in longevity and flexibility. HDPE offers high strength and resistance to punctures, while LDPE is more cost-effective and suitable for low-impact applications. Each type serves different needs based on performance and installation requirements.</p></p>
