<p><p>The Biological Seed Enhancement market comprises two main types: biofertilizers and biostimulants. Biofertilizers are natural organisms that enhance soil fertility and plant growth by fixing nitrogen, solubilizing phosphorus, and increasing nutrient availability. In contrast, biostimulants are substances or microorganisms that improve plant growth and health through mechanisms such as stress resistance and enhanced nutrient uptake. Both categories promote sustainable agriculture by reducing chemical inputs and improving crop yields while maintaining environmental balance.</p></p>
