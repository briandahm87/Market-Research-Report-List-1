<p><p>The isobutene market primarily involves two key segments: MTBE cracking and Tert-butanol (TBA) production. MTBE cracking refers to the process of breaking down methyl tert-butyl ether into its components, including isobutene, used as a high-octane fuel additive. Tert-butanol production involves the hydration of isobutene to create TBA, a solvent and chemical intermediate. Both segments reflect isobutene's versatility in petrochemical applications, with demand driven by the automotive and chemical industries for cleaner fuels and effective solvents.</p></p>
