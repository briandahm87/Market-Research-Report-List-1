<p><ul><li>Balloon</li><li>Lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nephrostomy catheters market is primarily divided into two types: balloon and lumen catheters. Balloon catheters feature a flexible balloon at the tip that can be inflated to maintain an open passage, enhancing drainage from the kidney. Lumen catheters, on the other hand, have a hollow tube designed for continuous drainage of urine from the kidney to an external collection bag. Both types are essential in managing urinary obstructions and renal conditions, ensuring effective kidney function preservation.</p></p>
