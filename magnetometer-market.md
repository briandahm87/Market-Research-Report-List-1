<p><p>The magnetometer market consists of various types based on their measurement capabilities. Single-axis magnetometers measure magnetic fields along one direction, making them simpler and less costly, suitable for basic applications. Three-axis magnetometers measure magnetic fields in three dimensions, providing a comprehensive understanding of magnetic environments, often used in navigation and geophysical applications. Three-dimensional magnetometers further enhance measurement precision and are typically employed in advanced applications like aerospace and mineral exploration, where detailed magnetic data is crucial.</p></p>
