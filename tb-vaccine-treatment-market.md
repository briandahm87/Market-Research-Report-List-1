<p><ul><li>Immune Vaccine</li><li>Therapy Vaccine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TB vaccine market primarily comprises two types: immune vaccines and therapy vaccines. Immune vaccines aim to prevent tuberculosis by enhancing the body's immune response against Mycobacterium tuberculosis. These vaccines are designed for high-risk populations to reduce infection rates. Therapy vaccines, on the other hand, focus on treating individuals already infected with TB by boosting their immune response to eliminate the bacteria. Together, these vaccine types play a crucial role in combating TB through prevention and treatment strategies.</p></p>
