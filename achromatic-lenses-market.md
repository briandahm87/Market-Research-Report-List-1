<p><ul><li>Positive Achromatic Lenses</li><li>Negative Achromatic Lenses</li><li>Cylindrical Achromatic Lenses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The achromatic lenses market consists of three main types: positive achromatic lenses, negative achromatic lenses, and cylindrical achromatic lenses. Positive achromatic lenses are designed to combine two or more lens elements to minimize chromatic aberration, enhancing image clarity. Negative achromatic lenses serve a similar purpose but diverge light, correcting distortion. Cylindrical achromatic lenses, on the other hand, focus on correcting distortions in one axis, making them ideal for applications requiring precision in specific directions, such as in laser systems and optical instruments.</p></p>
