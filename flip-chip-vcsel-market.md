<p><p>The Flip Chip VCSEL (Vertical-Cavity Surface-Emitting Laser) market is categorized into two primary types: single mode and multimode. Single mode VCSELs are designed for high precision and long-distance applications, offering a narrow beam and enhanced performance in data transmission. In contrast, multimode VCSELs support multiple light paths, making them suitable for short-range communications and higher data rates in applications like data centers. Both market types cater to diverse industries, including telecommunications and consumer electronics, for efficient optical connectivity.</p></p>
