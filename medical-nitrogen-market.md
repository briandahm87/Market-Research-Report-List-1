<p><p>The Medical Nitrogen Market is segmented into Gaseous State and Liquid State based on its physical form. Gaseous State nitrogen is widely used in medical applications like respiratory therapies, cryosurgery, and as a carrier gas in analytical instruments. It is stored in cylinders and is convenient for smaller-scale usage. Liquid State nitrogen, stored in cryogenic tanks, is primarily used for cryopreservation, freezing biological samples, and in advanced medical procedures. Both forms are essential, with the choice depending on specific medical requirements and storage capabilities.</p></p>
