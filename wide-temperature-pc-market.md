<p><ul><li>Fanless Wide Temperature PC</li><li>Fanned Wide Temperature PC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wide Temperature PC market comprises two primary types: Fanless Wide Temperature PCs and Fanned Wide Temperature PCs. Fanless Wide Temperature PCs are designed for extreme environments, utilizing passive cooling systems that enhance durability and reduce maintenance. These PCs are ideal for settings with dust, moisture, or vibrations. In contrast, Fanned Wide Temperature PCs employ active cooling through fans, allowing for higher processing power and better heat dissipation. They are suitable for applications that require robust performance in temperature-variable conditions.</p></p>
