<p><strong>The Interspinous Spacers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Compressible Spacers</li><li>Compressible Spacers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interspinous spacers market is categorized into two main types: non-compressible spacers and compressible spacers. Non-compressible spacers provide rigid support and maintain the intervertebral space, preventing nerve compression without changing their shape. In contrast, compressible spacers allow for some flexibility and adaptation under load, which can enhance comfort and dynamic movement. Both types aim to alleviate pain and improve spinal stability in patients with conditions like spinal stenosis, contributing to their growing adoption in orthopedic and neurosurgical practices.</p></p>
